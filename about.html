About Fronesis.

(ANCIENT GREEK: ΦΡΌΝΗΣΙΣ, PHRONĒSIS) MEANS PRACTICAL WISDOM.

Fronesis is dedicated to assisting creative individuals to expand their levels of awareness and influence.

Fronesis believes that the key for creativity is to have an on-going discipline of personal development to constantly push the boundaries of their own authenticity.

We provide the platform for creative individuals to manifest and materialize their expressions. Our consulting and events stimulate creativity in the mind by challenging blocks and paradigms that prevent growth. The result is an awakening of the creative genius that lies within each of our clients.
